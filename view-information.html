Is E-learning useful?


Join Forum Now See Speakers

About this

we will discuss the usefulness of whether e-learning is suitable for application in ASEAN.

Expert Speakers

These expert speakers offer diverse perspectives on the usefulness of e-learning, exploring its potential to enhance accessibility, empower lifelong learning, optimize educational experiences through data, engage digital natives, foster collaboration, and provide effective instructional design strategies.

What You'll Learn

Accessibility and Flexibility Lifelong Learning Empowerment Data-Driven Insights Engaging the Digital Natives Collaborative Learning in the Digital Era Strategies for Effective E-Learning Design

Speakers

Prof. John Thompson

Prof. Thompson, an expert in adult education, shares insights on how e-learning can foster lifelong learning. He explores the role of online courses, webinars, and virtual communities in empowering individuals to acquire new skills, adapt to changing job markets, and engage in continuous personal and professional development.

Prof. David Rodriguez

"Prof. Rodriguez, an expert in instructional design, explores the effectiveness of e-learning in engaging the millennial generation. He highlights the benefits of multimedia content, gamification, and social learning platforms in capturing the attention and active participation of digital natives, resulting in improved learning outcomes.

Prof. Mark Johnson

Prof. Johnson, an expert in instructional technology, shares practical strategies for designing effective e-learning experiences. He discusses the importance of clear learning objectives, interactive multimedia, and formative assessments in promoting learner engagement and knowledge retention. His talk provides valuable insights for educators transitioning to online teaching.

    ASEAN Youth Forum (AYF)

    The ASEAN Youth Forum (AYF) is a dynamic platform that empowers young people in the ASEAN region to engage in meaningful dialogue, cooperation, and action.
